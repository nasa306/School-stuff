About Us

Our mission is to provide a supportive and challenging learning environment that empowers middle school students to excel academically and personally in an ever-evolving modern age. We aim to teach relevant and practical skills with project-based learning, free of restrictions unfit for the modern age. We teach with a focus on teamwork and project-based grading. We employ later school starting times based on studies, Actual chefs that prepare food (Free, supported by donations), and advanced classes for the advanced. (Or those who just want to flex:))
