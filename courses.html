Courses

Core Classes: Basic English, Accelerated English, Advanced English, Basic Math, Accelerated Math, Advanced Math, Basic Science, Accelerated Science, Advanced Science, P.E., Basic History, Accelerated History, Advanced History
Access: Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays; Each teacher hosts their own access (not required but recommended), used for fun things or content support. Scheduling window is from Friday to Sunday.
To get into advanced classes, one must take a placement test. Accelerated/Basic classes are based off of own preference.
Students may come up with own clubs to get approved, find a teacher to sponsor either during access (BELOW) or after school
